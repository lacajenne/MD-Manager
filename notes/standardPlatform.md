
Standard Platform is no more. SimCorp One is the new standard. This also means that all the previous testing activities need
to be merged into the new standard. The role of the SK in this is crucial. The idea is that the SK provide a framework and support
so that teams can develop their own tests in the SimCorp One standard, run and observe them via reports.

We are not there yet. We want to avoid that the work of the SK keeps piling up because they have to follow their support projects
long after they have been delivered. 

Coming up with a standardization to onboard people to the SK testing framework is important.
