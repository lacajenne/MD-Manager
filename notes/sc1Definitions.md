Standard is the same for all clients. Everyone gets the same thing.

Parametrization means we offer a few different versions out of the box. Which version does the client get, e.g. which of the standard 3rd party FIX conection endpoints do they want, from our standard offerings.

Personalisation: client specific but within an area where we allow it within the standard, like defining the specific Parties and Portfolios that client uses.

Customisation: building completely new config that falls outside the other three descriptions.
