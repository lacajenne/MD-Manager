## Why This Role Is Important to Us

As a well-regarded company in our space, Simcorp is on a journey to transform from a traditional enterprise software company into a technology-enabled services business, while continuing to experience double-digit, highly profitable growth. To further support and drive this journey, we are transitioning to a horizontal delivery model, aligned to client outcomes.

We are now recruiting a manager for the team of engineers working across SimCorp One and Business Services Value Streams.

The team is responsible for day-to-day support of the value teams working in SimCorp One environment. The team is involved in the following activities:
- Implementation of test automation strategy and data
- First level tech support on infrastructure/tools/processes
- Continuous improvement initiatives, focusing on efficiency, quality, and cost reduction

This is a leadership role, requiring the ability to bridge long-term strategic vision with day-to-day delivery, ensuring that the solutions delivered are scalable, efficient, repeatable and built on clear standards.

The overall goal is to empower stream-aligned teams to become more proficient by bridging knowledge gaps through training, mentoring, and providing guidance on new tools and best practices.
